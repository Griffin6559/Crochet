Clothing


Creating clothes is one of the main uses of crochet! Crochet stitches hold a stiffer shape than knitting, but they can still make amazing clothes! In some situations, crochet's stiffer stitches can even be an advantage!
Stiffer stitches can create skirts that hold their shape, bigger sleeves on sweaters, bulkier jackets, and all sorts of accessories! Also, just like knitting, crochet can change colors to make all sorts of beautiful designs!
