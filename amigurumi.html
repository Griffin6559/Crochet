Amigurumi


Amigurumi is the what we call crocheted dolls/stuffed animals. Amigurumi is a Japanese word that is made up of "ami", which means to crochet/knit, and "gurumi", which means stuffed doll.
It is in amigurumi that you are going to see stitches like decreasing and increasing the most, since the stitches are used to make sphreres for heads, or other shapes for the bodies and clothing of the dolls.
Amigurumi dolls come in all shapes and sizes, and are my personal favorite thing to crochet!


Pica Pau

Pica Pau are some of my favorite books filled with patterns for adorable crochet animals. Each one of Yan Schenkel's creations have names, descriptions, and detailed patterns that may be long, but make it easy for a beginner!



You can make all of these and more!
