Crochet


Crochet is a popular fibre art that is used to create almost anything you can imagine! You can make clothing, stuffed animals (known as amigurmi), bookmarks,
cup holders, coasters, and whatever else you can imagine! Crochet is a medium through which you can create, it is unique to others in its category, like knitting and macrame.



Explore different types of crochet and learn how to get started!
